# CareerCraft – AI-Powered Personalized Career Guidance Assistant

## Overview

CareerCraft is an AI-powered career guidance assistant developed using IBM watsonx.ai Studio, IBM Granite Foundation Model, IBM watsonx Orchestrate, and IBM Cloud Lite.

The assistant interacts with users to understand their educational background, career goals, existing skills, learning preferences, and available study time. Based on this information, it generates personalized career recommendations, learning roadmaps, skill improvement suggestions, resume guidance, and interview preparation tips.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to choose the right career due to limited access to personalized guidance and awareness of industry requirements. CareerCraft addresses this challenge by providing intelligent, AI-driven career mentoring tailored to each user's profile.

---

## Technologies Used

- IBM watsonx.ai Studio
- IBM Granite Foundation Model (Granite 4-h-small)
- IBM watsonx Orchestrate
- IBM Cloud Lite

---

## Workflow

1. User enters career details and goals.
2. CareerCraft collects information through an interactive conversation.
3. IBM Granite analyzes the user's profile.
4. Personalized career recommendations are generated.
5. A structured learning roadmap is created.
6. Resume and interview guidance are provided.
